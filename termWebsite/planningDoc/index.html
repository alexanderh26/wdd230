Site Purpose

To promote and provide information about services provided by Temple Inn & Suites to temple patrons and their families who serve and/or participate in temple events.

Target Audience & Scenario

The target audience of this website is church members and their families that seek lodging while attending the temple for events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.
