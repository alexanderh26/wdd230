Site Plan

Site Name and Domain

My website will be called Rexburg Chamber of Commerce. The domain will be https://rexchamber.org

Site Purpose

The site purpose will be to provide resources for businesses within the Rexburg community and to help the communicate with one another. The goal of the Rexburg Chamber is to bring businesses together to help foster growth within the community, and I intend this website to help with that goal.

Target Audience & Scenario

The main audience for this website is business owners in the Rexburg area.
