Target Audience

Personas

The target audience can be broken down into specific personas. One Persona that represents the target audience is the young business owner in the Rexburg area. Another persona that might use the Chamber website would be members of chamber leadership who need to post announcements and use the website to keep in contact with the other members of Chamber.

Scenarios

Scenarios for visitors to land on this page might include things like wanting to register a new business with the chamber, wanting to pay chamber fees, wanting to be informed on chamber events, wanting to find ways to promote their business, and wanting to communicate with Chamber leadership.
